About Us

It's Not Just Food —
It's An Experience!

Cripsy Corner is a live snack mixing experience where we turn a simple chips packet into a delicious loaded snack. Customer chooses their favorite packet, and we open it, add fresh veggies, sauces, and our special masalas, mix it live, and serve it in the same packet.

Cripsy Corner is perfect for college fests, school events, birthday parties, exhibitions, and corporate events. Started by three college students, Cripsy Corner is not just food — it's an experience.

🌟
Live Experience

Watch your snack being made right in front of you — that's the magic!

🌿
Fresh Ingredients

Fresh veggies, premium sauces, and our special in-house masalas.

📦
Served in the Packet

No bowls, no plates — unique packet serving style that everyone loves!

Step by Step

How It Works

Six simple steps from plain packet to magic snack

01
🛍️

Pick Your Packet

Customer chooses their favorite chips packet — their brand, their call!

02
✂️

We Open It

We carefully open the top of the packet — this becomes your serving container!

03
🥗

Add Fresh Veggies

We load it with fresh chopped onions, cucumber, sweet corn, and more!

04
🫙

Add Sauces & Masala

Drizzle of mayo, schezwan sauce, and a generous sprinkle of our secret masala!

05
🤝

We Mix It

The magic moment — we shake and toss everything inside the packet, live!

06
🎉

Served In The Packet

Your loaded snack is ready — no bowl, no plate, just pure packet perfection!
